A bit about WTF Creative

Details

Digizine is a Wordpress Theme I designed and developed for my philosophy blog Theory Engine. It is inspired by hand made paper magazine ('zines) that became popular in the 1980s due to availablilty of mass reproduction or printed paper (Xerox/photocopy machines). These hand crafted pamphlets have become visually synonymous with grass roots activism and social change. Zine making remains a popular activity today.
