A bit about Nomadic.fm

Details

I really enjoyed building the marketing site for startup Nomadic.fm from a beautiful design by their co-founder Tim. The designs were in Photoshop which I translated into modern CSS and HTML with responsiveness as standard. The designs were optimised for IPAD landscape dimensions and I used my discretion to write appropriate media queries over and above the Bootstrap framework to optimise the site for all devices. This included a mobile menu for small screens and a constrained viewport for extra large screens. The work was a start to finish project and took just over a week to complete.
